Ayurvedic Remedies and Medicines

1. Common Cold / Cough

An excess of Pitta and Kapha causes cold and cough. To tackle this, Tulsi tea, Mulethi tea, Honey-Ginger tea, Pippali churna, Sonth goli/Sonth with Honey, Cinnamon tea/Cinnamon with Honey and Giloy juice/Giloy tablet are some ayurvedic remedies to manage your cold and cough.

2. Fever

There are thirteen types of fever described in Ayurveda caused due different imbalances of the three doshas. Tulsi tea, Garlic tea, Honey-Ginger tea, Cilantro leaves juice, and Cinnamon tea/Cinnamon with Honey are some ayurvedic remedies to manage your fever.

3. Head Ache

The main cause of pitta type headaches are dehydration so sipping into water can also cure your headache, other than this putting a few drops of Brahmi and Ghee in the nostrils may sooth head aches. Chandan paste, Tagar tea, Choti elaichi, Rock Salt are also some remedies for your headache.

Headaches are also classified as Vata, Kapha and Pitta type. Vata type headaches are usually in the back portion of the head, putting warm ghee in each nostrils and and a glass for water with salt and sugar may help in this. Pitta type headaches originate from temple area and travels to the central part of the head, taking aloe vera gel or applying chandan paste on forehead can help in this. Kapha type headaches are typically the ones that occur during the winter or spring season. It often strikes in morning or evening and tends to get worse as you bend down, applying ginger paste on forehead may help in this.

4. Hair Loss

Hair Loss is related to aggravation of Pitta Dosha, it could be caused due to weight loss, abysmal iron levels, irregular diet, stress and thyroid or hormonal imbalances. Massaging Lime juice and Amla powder paste on scalp and hair, applying Bhringraj oil, massaging scalp with Shikakai powder in Coconut oil, Reetha shampoo, Coconut milk/Coconut oil, Aloe Vera pulp, Methi paste, Jojoba hair oil, Yogurt with Honey and Lemon juice, Neem paste, and natural oils such as Rosemerry oil, Lavendar oil, Juniper oil, Castor oil can help reducing hair fall and stimulate hair growth.

5. Cystic Acne

Acnes are caused due to imapairment of Agni which leads to imbalance of all three Doshas predominantly the Pitta Dosha. It can also be caused due to excessive intake of oily, spicy, salty and sour foods. Drinking Amla juice daily, taking herbal mixture of powdered Coriander seeds, Turmeric, Fennel, Basil, and Amla 15 minutes before lunch and dinner, taking herbal mixture of Kutki, Guduchi and Shatavari 2 to 3 times a day, applying Neeem paste on skin, applying Melons on skin, and drinking Cumin, Corriander and Fennel tea can help treat Cystic acne.

6. Diarrhea

Diarrhea is termed as Atisara in Ayurveda, it could be caused by intake of foods of any particular quality in excess; foods incompatible with our prakriti or with seasonal variations; intake of foods that are not well cooked; contaminated water or other beverages, etc. In Ayurveda, diarrhoea is also described to be induced by mental causes and by toxins. Atisar is classified into 7 distinct types, namely Vata, Pitta, Kapha, Bhaya (caused by fear), Tridosha, Shoka (caused by grief) and Raktatisara (diarrhoea associated with bleeding). Intake of Whey, Buttermilk, Raw Banana, Poppy seeds and Pomagranate can help manage this condition.

7. Vomiting

Vomiting or Nausea could be caused due to motion sickness, morning sickness, pregnancy, excess acid secretion, worms in colon, food poisoning or flu. Drinking Lemonade, chewing Cardamom seeds, Taking Ginger juice/Ginger pulp and Onion juice, Cumin seeds and Nutmeg steeped tea and drinking mixture of equal parts of lemon juice and honey can help manage the symptoms.

8. PCOS

PCOS or Polycystic Ovary Syndrome is a condition where women develop small cysts in their ovaries. It is not a serious or harmful condition, but it does cause hormonal changes in women. The multiple cysts on the ovaries lead to excessive hormone production particularly androgen, which leads to male tendencies, internally and externally. Exercising regularly, maintaining healthy diet like avoiding high calorie food, eating small and frequent meals, taking nourishing breakfast, adding fibrous food in meal. Maintaing a stable ovulation cycle, keeping mind and body stress free and eating special diets can help manage PCOS. Involving Yoga like Pranayam and Asanas in daily schedule helps tone up the whole Reproductive System and help manage its disorders.

9. Hypertension

Hypertension is triggered due to exacerbated Vata dosha leading to disharmony in the three bodily Doshas of Vata, Pitta and Kapha. Consuming powder of bark extracts of Arjuna, Amla/Amla juice, Punarnava formulations, Sarpagandha formulations, Moong Dal soup, Peach juice with Coriander and Cardamom powder, Orange juice, Coconut Water, Honey-Water, Cucumber Raita, Tulsi leaves/Tulsi tea can help manage hypertension and bring high bloop pressure back to normal range.

10. Tooth Ache

Toothache can be caused due to decay of teeth that is breakdown or destruction of the tooth structure by deminerlisation. Various Ayurvedic remedies such as Clove/Clove oil, Garlic paste with salt and pepper, Raw Onion, Asafetida with Lemon juice, Ginger paste with Cayenne pepper and olive oil, Peppermint oil with olive oil, Warm water with Sea salt, Cucumber, and Turmeric with warm Musturd oil can be used to reduce the pain.

11. Weak Immunity

Immunity is a concept explained in Ayurveda under multiple topics. The most important ones are Bala or the concept of strength, Vyadhi Kshamathwa or the concept of resistance to illness development, and Ojas or the concept of supreme resilience. The concept of Bala explains the ability of the system to repair and nourish itself and be effective in disease prevention, whereas Vyadhi Kshamatwa is the ability of the immune system to fight against the disease-causing pathogens.

Ayurvedic remedies such as Tulsi, Pippali and Ginger tea (strengthen and cleanse the upper respiratory tract.), Turmeric and Pepper in meals (reduces toxins in body), Amla juice or Chyavanprash (supplies extra antioxidants and strengthens immunity), Warm water (keeps throat and mucous membrane moist), helps to strengthen the Immune System.
